Heather Rooney

Heather Rooney is a renowned American artist celebrated for her photorealistic colored pencil and graphite drawings. Born on August 31, 1993, in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, she developed a passion for art during high school, where she became captivated by the challenge of photorealistic drawing. Rooney gained widespread recognition by sharing time-lapse videos of her drawing process on platforms like YouTube, Instagram, and Tumblr. Her videos have garnered millions of views, with her "Drawing Harry Potter" video alone reaching over 10 million views. (Reference: Famous Birthdays)
