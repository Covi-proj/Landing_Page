About me

As an Artist

In 2015, at the age of 13, I started drawing using graphite. I've concentrated upon realism over the years, always challenging myself to enhance the intricacies and complexity of my work. I eventually started drawing digitally as well, broadening my skill set beyond conventional media. Whether using a pencil or a laptop, I enjoy capturing reality, and I'm constantly trying to improve my methods. For me, creating art is more than simply a pastime; I've spent years doing it and am constantly looking to improve. I'm always looking for new ways to express myself and share my work with others.
(Check out my Instagram page for more of my artwork.)

As a Developer

In 2022, I began programming with an emphasis on UI/UX design and front-end development. My work focuses on designing simple, intuitive user interfaces that strike a balance between style and usefulness. I have practical experience using front-end technologies to make designs come to life and Figma for UI/UX design. To guarantee a seamless user experience, I give usability, responsiveness, and performance top priority. I've been learning new technologies and honing my abilities to create better, more effective applications ever since I started programming.
